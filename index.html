Intro

Experience the magic of KőSuli, where school kids rock the stage with theatrical and improvisational approaches, inspired by the legendary Frank Zappa's music and his band in the 70's. Work

Get ready to be blown away by KőSuli's unique performances, where school kids showcase their incredible talent. Our band draws inspiration from the creativity of Frank Zappa's music and his 70's band, delivering unforgettable experiences on stage.

Andrew J. Shepherd, the mastermind behind KőSuli, is not just a musician but also a renowned chef and IT expert. His passion for music and dedication to nurturing young talent have made KőSuli a rising star in the music scene. Join us on this extraordinary musical journey!

About

KőSuli is on a mission to revolutionize the music world by showcasing the incredible talents of school kids. Our performances are a blend of theatricality and improvisational genius, reminiscent of Frank Zappa's groundbreaking music and his band's 70's era.

Andrew J. Shepherd, the driving force behind KőSuli, is not just a musical virtuoso but also a talented chef and IT expert. His diverse skills and passion for music have transformed KőSuli into a powerhouse of creativity. Join us and be part of this musical revolution!
